Background Page

Quiet money cartel.

Santa Fortuna prefers clean operations, bribery energy, and control that does not need to announce itself.

The profile

The cartel coordinates supply routes, runs discreet meetings, enforces debts, and uses proxies when a message needs distance. Violence is not the brand. Profit is.

Typical Play

Planning First

Operations are mapped before engines start. Scouts check approaches, muscle holds overwatch, and negotiators keep the meeting pointed at the outcome.

Prevention

Ambush Control

Santa Fortuna does not trust luck. Meet locations are screened, escape routes are assigned, and every convoy has a reason to look ordinary.

Leverage

Negotiation

Debts, favors, secrets, and timing create pressure. The strongest move is the one that makes the other side agree before the threat is spoken.

Signature

Strict Comms

Short calls, clean channels, masked muscle, and no pointless wars. Noise is expensive, and Santa Fortuna does not pay for waste.
